# Cold email campaign plan

## Objective
Start 25-50 highly targeted conversations with small agencies and learn whether SignalSift's sample offer creates replies and curiosity.

## Batch 1 size
25 agencies maximum.

## Why keep batch 1 small
- faster manual personalization
- easier to learn from replies
- avoids blasting weak messaging before it is proven

## Sequence
### Email 1
Short intro + free sample offer

### Follow-up 1 (3-4 days later)
Bump with one concrete example signal

### Follow-up 2 (5-7 days later)
Short break-up / low-pressure close

## Personalization rules
Include at least one of:
- agency niche
- city / geography
- service mix
- a line about who they serve

## Offer framing
Do not pitch software first.
Pitch a **free signal sample** first.

## Success criteria for batch 1
- 3+ replies
- 1-2 sample requests
- at least one useful objection that sharpens positioning

## Failure criteria
- no replies at all
- replies indicate confusion about who this is for
- offer feels too abstract

## Iteration plan
If batch 1 underperforms:
1. tighten subject lines
2. tighten niche focus
3. make sample outputs more concrete
4. narrow to a single agency subtype (likely SEO agencies)
