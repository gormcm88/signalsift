# Sample fulfillment workflow

## Goal
Deliver a useful free sample quickly enough that interested agencies actually remember requesting it.

## Intake
- receive request via project inbox
- confirm agency type and service sold
- collect 5-10 target companies if not already included

## Fulfillment steps
1. Add requested targets to watchlist
2. Run monitor
3. Review alerts manually
4. Curate the 3-5 strongest signals
5. Rewrite into concise agency-friendly notes
6. Send sample back with short explanation and CTA

## Sample response structure
- 1 sentence on what SignalSift does
- 3-5 example prospect signals
- 1-2 suggested outreach angles
- ask if they want ongoing monitoring

## SLA target
Respond within 24 hours while validation is fresh.

## Important
This phase is manually assisted. That is acceptable. Speed and usefulness matter more than purity.
