# Objection handling

## "We can just do this manually"
Totally fair. The question is whether your team actually does it consistently enough across enough prospects to find good timing windows before competitors do.

## "We already use Apollo / Clay / ZoomInfo"
That makes sense. SignalSift is not trying to replace your contact database. It is meant to add better timing and better reasons-to-reach-out on top of your existing prospecting workflow.

## "This feels too early"
It is early. That’s why I’m offering a free sample first — so you can judge the usefulness from real accounts instead of guessing.

## "Not sure the output would be good enough"
That’s exactly what the sample is for. If the examples aren’t useful, you shouldn’t pay for it.

## "We do mostly inbound"
Then this is probably less urgent for you. It’s strongest for agencies that still do active outbound or strategic prospecting.
