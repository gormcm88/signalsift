What SignalSift catches

The point is not “more data.” It’s noticing business change that suggests urgency, growth, or a new opening for an agency pitch.

Offer and positioning changes

New homepage language, new service lines, pricing pages, or repositioning.

Expansion signals

New locations, service-area pages, hiring pages, or commercial service launches.

Reputation shifts

Review drops or spikes that create urgency around conversion and reputation management.

Agency-ready angles

Signals are translated into a usable reason to contact a prospect — not just a raw diff.
