# Targeting criteria — Phase 1

## Primary ICP
Small agencies serving SMBs.

## Best-fit subtypes
- SEO agencies
- Paid media agencies
- Lead generation agencies
- Web / CRO agencies

## Best-fit characteristics
- small team (roughly 1-25 people)
- visibly does outbound or business development
- serves local businesses or SMBs
- has clear niche or service specialization
- likely to understand the value of better prospect timing

## Avoid for now
- very large agencies with layered procurement
- agencies focused purely on branding/creative retainers
- enterprise consultancies
- agencies that do no outbound at all

## Good signs on their website
- case studies
- niche focus
- clear service pages
- founder-led agency
- call booking CTA
- emphasis on lead generation / growth / ROI

## Suggested first list size
25-50 agencies for the first manual validation batch.
