SignalSift for agencies

Give your team a better reason to reach out than “just following up.”

SignalSift helps agencies spot businesses that changed something important — new offers, new service pages, expansion signals, hiring activity, or reputation shifts — and turns those changes into outreach angles that feel timely instead of random.

Local SEO / web / CRO agency

A dental prospect changes homepage messaging toward emergency visits, adds a new patient offer, and sees reviews slip. That is a materially better trigger for outreach than “they fit our ICP.”
