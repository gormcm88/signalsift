# Phase 1 launch plan

## Objective
Get the first real conversations with agencies and learn whether SignalSift's output is compelling enough to justify a monthly subscription.

## Website role
The live website is a credibility layer and explanation tool, not a full self-serve SaaS yet.

## Offer
Free signal sample for 5-10 prospect accounts.

## Channel priority
1. Cold outbound to small agencies
2. LinkedIn posts + DMs
3. X posts with signal examples
4. Agency communities once copy/output is tighter

## What to measure
- replies
- sample requests
- actual sample fulfillment interest
- willingness to pay after seeing examples
- objections

## Immediate next improvements
- create a target-account sourcing list
- create 3 sample outputs by niche
- refine outreach copy after first responses
- move from email intake to a proper backend if response volume justifies it
