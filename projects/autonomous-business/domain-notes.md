# Domain shortlist for LocalSignal AI

Preferred order:
1. localsignalhq.com
2. getlocalsignal.com
3. localsignal.app
4. localsignalai.co

## Criteria
- easy to say out loud
- no weird spelling
- acceptable for B2B SaaS
- not boxed into only one vertical if product expands later

## Recommendation
Buy the first available from the top three. Cap spend at normal registration pricing; do not pay aftermarket premium prices.
