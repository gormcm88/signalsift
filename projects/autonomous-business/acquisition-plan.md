# Acquisition Plan

## Objective
Get the first 3 paying customers as fast as possible using low-cost, repeatable channels.

## ICP
1. Solo agents in mid-to-large metros
2. Small brokerages with 5-50 agents
3. Loan officers or local real estate service businesses who need market content

## Offer
Free sample report for their city + brand voice.

## Outreach angles
- "Look like the local expert without spending hours writing content"
- "Turn market data into weekly posts and newsletters automatically"
- "Give every agent in your brokerage a content engine"

## Channel 1: cold email
- scrape or source agent contacts from public websites/directories once approved
- send short personalized email + link to free sample
- CTA: reply with city + brand name or use form

## Channel 2: programmatic SEO
- city-specific landing pages: "[City] real estate market content for agents"
- free sample pages targeting local intent

## Channel 3: partnership/referral
- offer white-label / brokerage version
- first month discount for team rollouts

## First 3-customer milestone
- 100 targeted leads
- 20-30 sample requests
- 3 conversions to paid plan

## Metrics to watch
- visitor to lead conversion rate
- lead to demo/sample completion rate
- sample to paid conversion rate
- churn after first month

## Biggest risks
- generic output that feels too AI-written
- weak distribution
- unreliable underlying market data

## Mitigation
- keep outputs data-backed and local
- niche into specific geographies or brokerage segments
- make onboarding dead simple
