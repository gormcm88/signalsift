## Winner
# SignalSift

## Why SignalSift wins
- broad B2B TAM
- recurring pain with clear revenue linkage
- faster path to valuable alerts than full workflow products
- easier to sell on ROI than creative/content output
- can start narrow and expand horizontally

## Initial ICP
- small agencies selling web/SEO/ads
- recruiters / staffing operators
- fractional sales / SDR shops
- consultants targeting SMBs

## MVP wedge
Instead of trying to be "Clay for everyone," start with **simple high-value signals**:
- company launched a new location page
- company is hiring for marketing/sales roles
- rating dropped sharply or review volume spiked
- homepage/title/offer changed
- new funding / leadership / job-change mentions if easy to source

## Pricing hypothesis
- Starter: $49/mo — 25 tracked companies, daily alerts
- Pro: $149/mo — 100 tracked companies, enriched summaries
- Agency: $399/mo — 500 tracked companies, multi-client workspaces

## Path to $10k/mo
- 205 customers at $49/mo
- 68 customers at $149/mo
- 26 customers at $399/mo
- or some mix

## Build sequence
1. Landing page
2. Manual company watchlist input
3. Monitor pages + changes + review deltas
4. Alert summaries
5. Dashboard + email digests
6. Niche-specific positioning pages
