# Implementation Plan

## Goal
Launch an autonomous online business capable of reaching $10,000/month recurring revenue with less than $500 in startup cost.

## Chosen business
LocalSignal AI: automated local market reports and marketing content for real estate professionals.

## Phase 1 — MVP (days 1-3)
- Create landing page
- Create sample report generator
- Create pricing and demo offer
- Set up lead capture

## Phase 2 — Fulfillment automation (days 3-7)
- Inputs:
  - location
  - brokerage/agent name
  - tone
  - audience
- Pipeline:
  - fetch market data
  - compute narrative bullets
  - generate content assets
  - email/report delivery

## Phase 3 — Distribution (days 5-14)
- Programmatic SEO pages for city names
- Free report lead magnet
- Outreach to agents and brokerages
- Referral offer
